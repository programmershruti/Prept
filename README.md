# Prept – AI-Powered Mock Interview Platform

Prept is a full-stack interview preparation platform that connects interviewees with experienced engineers for mock interviews. The platform includes AI-generated interview questions, video calling, live chat, booking management, feedback reports, and credit-based subscriptions.

## Features

### For Interviewees

* Browse and discover interviewers
* Book mock interview sessions
* Join HD video interviews
* Receive AI-powered feedback reports
* Access interview recordings (plan-based)

### For Interviewers

* Create professional interviewer profiles
* Manage availability slots
* Conduct live interviews
* Earn credits from booked sessions
* Generate AI interview questions during calls

### AI Features

* AI-generated interview questions
* AI-powered interview feedback
* Candidate performance analysis
* Improvement recommendations
